Standalone Scripts

With Rules, Profiles and Transformations you can handle about 98% of your automation needs. Very advanced, unusual or complex scenarios might require a scripted solution though. A Script is more powerful, but also harder to edit, maintain and process for openHAB.

Multiple programming languages are supported: Jython (Python 2.6 dialect), JavaScript, or Groovy (Java dialect).
